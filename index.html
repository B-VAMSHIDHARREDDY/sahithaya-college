About Us

Empowering Rayalaseema’s
Next Generation

Sahithya Degree & PG College stands as a beacon of quality higher education in Khajipet Sunkesula, serving students from across Kadapa district and the wider Rayalaseema region. We deliver accessible, rigorous and transformative education that prepares students for careers, competitive exams and life.

Start Your Journey
With Us

Seats are limited. Follow the simple steps and secure your place at Sahithya Degree & PG College.

1

Fill Application Form

Submit the enquiry or visit the office. Bring your 10+2 / Degree mark sheets and Aadhaar card.

2

Document Verification

Originals: mark sheets, Transfer Certificate, caste certificate (if applicable) and passport photos.

3

Fee Payment & Enrolment

Pay admission fee, receive student ID, hall ticket and course timetable.

4

Orientation & Classes Begin

Meet faculty, attend orientation and start classes. Government scholarships available.
