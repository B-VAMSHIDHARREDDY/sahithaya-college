HOW TO USE
──────────
1. Open index.html in any modern browser (Chrome/Edge/Firefox)
2. To host: upload index.html to Netlify, GitHub Pages,
   Hostinger or any web host. No server needed.

WHERE TO REPLACE DUMMY CONTENT
───────────────────────────────
PHOTOS: Every dummy image card shows "Replace with photo"
  → Replace the entire <div class="dummy-img">...</div>
    block with: <img src="your-photo.jpg" style="width:100%;height:100%;object-fit:cover;"/>

VIDEO: The campus tour video section
  → In the loadVideo() JS function, replace the YouTube
    src URL with your own YouTube video embed URL:
    https://www.youtube.com/embed/YOUR_VIDEO_ID

FACULTY PHOTOS: Replace the emoji avatar (👨‍💼) in
  <div class="fty-avatar"> with an <img> tag.
  Add real names, titles and descriptions in fty-body.

PHONE NUMBER: Search "08563" and replace with real number.
